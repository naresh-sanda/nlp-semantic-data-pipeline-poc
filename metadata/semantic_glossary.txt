Term: Churn
Definition: The rate at which customers stop doing business with an entity.

Term: AOV
Definition: Average Order Value. Total revenue divided by the number of orders.

Term: ERP
Definition: Enterprise Resource Planning. The source system where transactional data is generated (e.g., Salesforce, Epicor, Priority ERP, SYSPRO).

Term: Dimension
Definition: A categorical variable used to slice and dice data, such as Region, Customer, or Product.

Term: Metric
Definition: A quantifiable measure that is used to track and assess the status of a specific business process.
