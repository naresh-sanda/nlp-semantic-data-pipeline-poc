Rule 1: Revenue Calculation
Revenue should always be calculated using the net amount after discounts. Do not include taxes in the revenue calculation.

Rule 2: Profit Margin
Profit Margin is calculated as (Revenue - Cost) / Revenue. It should always be represented as a percentage.

Rule 3: Active Customers
A customer is considered 'Active' if they have placed an order within the last 12 months.

Rule 4: Top Customers
When calculating 'Top Customers', always sort by Revenue descending unless specified otherwise.

Rule 5: Region Hierarchy
Regions roll up to Countries, which roll up to Continents. When analyzing by region, default to the lowest level available in the dataset.

Rule 6: Churn Definition
Customer churn happens when an active customer does not place an order for 18 consecutive months.
